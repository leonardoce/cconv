iConv is a software that can convert coordinate into the supported reference systems. iConv, also, maintains a translations report who contains all the transformation data. You can also acquire a fix from the GPS system evaluating the satellite status and the intensity of the signal.

The precision of the fixes acquired with the GPS system depends on the sensibility of your GPS and on the athmospheric conditions.

The supported reference systems are:

  • Cassini-Soldner (italian land register system);
  • Gauss-Boaga;
  • Latitude and Longitude;
  • UTM timezone 32,33,34, using datum ED50 and WGS84;
  • MGRS;
  • Web Mercator.

The main functionalities are:

  • transformation between a reference system and another;
  • fix acquisition from GPS;
  • configuration of the emission origin of the Cassini-Soldner system;
  • Gauss-Boaga timezone configuration;
  • transformation registry that can be saved as HTML and CSV.

Compass manual

The quality of the received data depends on the quality of the sensors inside your smartphone.

If you see that the calculated direction has big errors you can follow a procedure to reset your sensors.
This procedure consist in pointing your smartphone toward the sky and drawing an 8 in the air.
If you have tried this procedure and the sensors aren't working you can rotate your smartphone 360 degree and turn it upside down.

The magnetic sensor can be troubled by near metallic objects. For this reason under the compass we have displayed the power of the electro-magnetic field recorded. The natural power is between 40uT and 50uT. If you are near a metallic object this values will grow bigger.
If the power of the electro-magnetic field is too high the power of the electro-magnetic field if drew in red and this means that the direction recorded can be wrong.
